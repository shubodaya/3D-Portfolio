About Me

About Me



Hi, I’m Shubodaya - a tech-driven problem solver with a strong passion for networks and security. I thrive in environments where infrastructure, performance, and protection come together to create reliable, resilient systems. With hands-on experience in configuring firewalls, setting up secure VPNs, monitoring systems, and responding to real-time threats, I enjoy diving deep into how things connect and more importantly, how to keep them safe. My background spans both enterprise and cloud-based environments, where I’ve worked with tools like SonicWall, Splunk, Wireshark, Microsoft Defender, and more. I hold an MSc in Cybersecurity (Distinction, NCSC-certified) from Swansea University, where I focused on topics like secure architectures, intrusion detection, and vulnerability management. Before that, I earned my engineering degree in Electronics and Communication, which laid the groundwork for my understanding of systems, protocols, and critical thinking. Whether it's building secure infrastructure, automating routine processes, or investigating unusual activity across a network, I bring curiosity, precision, and a readiness to tackle complex technical challenges. I'm also someone who values documentation, collaboration, and lifelong learning because in tech, staying still is never an option. Outside of work, I channel my creativity into web design, sports, photography, and the occasional jam session with my guitar. I’m always eager to take on new challenges, learn, and grow in the ever-evolving field of cybersecurity. Let’s connect and build a safer digital world together!
